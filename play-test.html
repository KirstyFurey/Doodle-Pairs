How to play

  1. Select the number of cards to match in 'Play Mode' and decide if you want to play in 'ridiculous' mode.
  2. Hit start to begin and start timer.
  3. Click/tap on a card to flip it face up. Click/tap the card again to return it face down - this is pretty important in 'ridiculous' mode!
  4. Match a pair of cards to remove them from the board.
  5. If you're playing in 'ridiculous' mode and you flip a pair that does not match the entire board will re-shuffle - so be careful!
  6. Once all the cards are matched, YOU WIN! and your time is shown. Why not try to beat it, or take on the challenge of 'ridiculous' mode!
